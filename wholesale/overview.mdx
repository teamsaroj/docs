---

Wholesale shows tagged partners their price exactly where your theme shows the regular
price, and charges it automatically at checkout. An optional AI assistant lets partners
negotiate — within limits you set.

## Who it's for

Stores that sell to both retail shoppers and **wholesale partners** from the same catalog,
without maintaining a separate storefront or messy discount codes.
