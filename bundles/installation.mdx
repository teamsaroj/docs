---
title: "Installation"
description: "Install Porttera Bundles, choose a plan, and get your first bundle live."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install from the Shopify App Store">
    Search for **Porttera Bundles** in the Shopify App Store, or open the listing link
    directly, and click **Install**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the permissions">
    Shopify shows a permissions screen before the install finishes. Porttera Bundles asks for:

    - **Read and write products** — to show your catalog in the product picker and, when
      Subscribe & Save is on, to attach the subscription plan to the variants you've chosen.
    - **Read and write discounts** — to create the automatic discount that prices bundles at
      checkout.
    - **Read and write metaobjects (and their definitions)** — bundle configuration is stored
      as metaobjects on your own store, not in some database you don't control.

    It doesn't ask for order, customer, or storefront-content access, because it doesn't need
    any of that to work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    You'll land on **Plan & billing** inside the app. There are two plans, both with a
    7-day free trial:

    | Plan | Price | Included |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | Basic | $9.99/mo | Up to 5 bundles, fixed-price and tiered pricing |
    | Pro | $24.99/mo | Unlimited bundles, always-included items, priority support |

    Picking a plan sends you to Shopify's own hosted pricing page — the charge shows up on
    your normal Shopify bill, and you can change or cancel the plan any time from
    **Settings → Billing** in your Shopify admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the app home checklist">
    The app's home page walks through what's left before you're live: a plan active, the
    automatic bundle-pricing discount active, and at least one bundle created. If the pricing
    discount shows as not active yet, see
    [Troubleshooting](/bundles/troubleshooting#the-bundle-pricing-discount-isnt-active).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first bundle">
    Go to **Bundles → Create bundle**, give it a title, choose fixed or tiered pricing, and
    add at least one category of products (or some always-included items). Save. The full
    field-by-field walkthrough is in [Configuration](/bundles/configuration).

    <Frame caption="Creating a bundle in the app admin">
      <img src="/images/bundles-setup.svg" alt="Create a bundle" />
    </Frame>

    Saving gives the bundle a **handle** — you'll need it in the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the builder block to your theme">
    Open **Online Store → Themes → Customize**, go to the page or template where you want the
    bundle to show, click **Add block**, and choose **Bundle builder**. Paste the bundle's
    handle into the block's **Bundle handles** setting. Save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Open the page, pick items until the bundle is complete, and add it to the cart. Confirm the
    cart total matches the bundle price (or the discounted total, for tiered bundles) before
    you publish the page to real traffic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  A bundle is live once its handle is in a published theme block and the cart total comes out
  right. If it doesn't, [Troubleshooting](/bundles/troubleshooting) covers the common causes.
</Check>
