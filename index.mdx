Small Shopify apps that each do one job and stay out of your theme's way. Pick an app below,
or start with how they install.

<Note>
  Every Porttera app installs as a Shopify **theme app extension** — a clean block you add in
  the theme editor. No code is injected into your theme, and uninstalling leaves nothing behind.
</Note>
