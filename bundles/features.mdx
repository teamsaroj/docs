## Fixed and tiered pricing

Set one flat price for a defined set of items, or let customers add any quantity and earn a
bigger percentage off as they add more. Both live side by side — different bundles can use
different pricing types.

## Categories with pick counts

Split a bundle into categories ("Sauces," "T-shirts," whatever fits) and require a specific
number of picks from each one, instead of one flat product list.

## Free-gift categories

Mark a category as a free gift and everything picked from it is added at no charge, while the
rest of the bundle keeps its normal price.

## Always-included items

Add products that go into every order of a bundle automatically, with no picking required —
useful for a fixed, preselected set, or for something you want bundled with every purchase.

## Subscribe & Save

Turn on a subscribe option per bundle. Subscribers get 15% off instead of the bundle's usual
discount and a recurring delivery every 30 days, run through a Shopify selling plan the app
manages for you.

## Multiple bundles, one block

List more than one bundle handle in a single theme block and it renders as tabs, so a page can
offer several build-your-own sets without adding a block per bundle.

## Real variants, one add-to-cart

Every item a shopper picks is added to the cart as its actual product variant, in one atomic
request. Nothing is combined into a single fake "bundle SKU" — inventory, fulfillment, and
your reports all see the real products.

## Pricing enforced at checkout, not just on the page

The bundle discount runs as a Shopify Function on the cart, so the price a shopper sees is the
price they're charged — it isn't just a number shown on a page that a direct checkout link
could skip.

## Drop-off analytics

See where shoppers stop building a bundle — opened, part way through, or added to cart — plus
bundle order counts and sales once orders start coming in.

## Any Online Store 2.0 theme

The builder is a theme app extension. It doesn't touch `theme.liquid`, doesn't need
theme-specific support, and follows your theme's own colors and corner radius automatically.
