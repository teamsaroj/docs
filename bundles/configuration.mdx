All of this lives in the app admin — under **Apps → Porttera Bundles** in your Shopify admin —
not in the theme editor. The theme editor only holds the display settings, covered in
[Customization](/bundles/customization).

## The bundle editor

Go to **Bundles → Create bundle** (or open an existing one to edit it). Every bundle has these
fields:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bundle title" icon="heading">
    Shown to customers in the cart and as the tab label if a page shows more than one bundle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Description" icon="align-left">
    Shown under the title on the storefront. Optional.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Label" icon="tag">
    A short badge shown above the bundle's tab — e.g. "Free shipping" or "Best value."
    Optional.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pricing type" icon="dollar-sign">
    **Fixed bundle price** — the whole bundle costs one set price, no matter which items are
    picked.

    **Tiered volume discount** — there's no fixed price. Customers add any quantity, and the
    percentage off goes up at the item counts you define in the tiers below.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bundle price (fixed only)" icon="sack-dollar">
    What the whole bundle should cost. This only applies to fixed bundles and it has to be
    greater than $0.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Discount tiers (tiered only)" icon="chart-line">
    Each tier is an item count and a percent off — e.g. 3 items = 10% off, 5 items = 15% off,
    10 items = 25% off. Add as many tiers as you want; the highest tier a customer's cart
    qualifies for is the one that applies. Switching pricing type to tiered for the first time
    prefills those three example tiers so you have something to start from.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Offer Subscribe & Save" icon="rotate">
    Turns on a one-time / subscribe toggle on the storefront. Choosing subscribe gives the
    customer 15% off (instead of the bundle's normal discount) and sets up a recurring
    delivery every 30 days, through a Shopify selling plan the app creates and manages for
    you — there's nothing to configure in Shopify's own subscriptions settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Categories

A bundle is built from one or more categories. Click **Add category**, give it a name (e.g.
"Sauces," "T-shirts"), and set:

- **Items to pick** — how many items from this category a customer must choose. This only
  shows for fixed bundles; tiered bundles let customers pick any quantity from each category.
- **Free gift (100% off)** — turns this category into a pick-a-free-gift category. Items
  picked here are added at no charge; everything else in the bundle keeps its normal price.

Click **Add products** to open Shopify's product picker and add items to the category. Each
item shows in a list underneath, with a **Remove** link if you want to take it back out.

You can add as many categories as you like, and remove a whole category with the **Remove
category** button in its header.

### Always-included items

Below the categories is a separate list for items that go into every order of this bundle
automatically — no picking required. A bundle made up only of always-included items (no
categories) works as a fixed, preselected set.

## Saving

**Save** validates the bundle before it writes anything:

- A tiered bundle needs at least one category with products **and** at least one discount
  tier.
- A fixed bundle needs either a category with products or some always-included items, **and**
  a bundle price greater than $0.

Once it saves, the bundle gets a **handle** shown at the top of the page — that's what you
paste into the theme block's settings (see [Customization](/bundles/customization)).

## The Bundles list

**Bundles** in the app's sidebar lists every bundle with its title, price, and pick count. Open
one to edit it, or use its **Delete** button to remove it (you'll get a confirmation prompt
first — deleting is immediate and can't be undone from the app).

## Analytics

The **Analytics** page shows a drop-off funnel for each bundle — how many shoppers open the
builder, how many get through picking, and how many finish — over the last 30 days, plus
bundle order counts and sales over a longer window once you have orders coming in. There's
nothing to configure here; it fills in as customers use the builder.

## App settings

The **Settings** page in the app has two things on it:

- **Open theme editor** — a shortcut straight to your theme's customizer, so you don't have to
  go hunting for it in your Shopify admin.
- **Re-create definitions** — rebuilds the metaobject definitions the app relies on to store
  bundle data. You shouldn't need this normally; it's there for the rare case where bundles
  stop loading in the theme editor (see
  [Troubleshooting](/bundles/troubleshooting)).

## Plan & billing

**Plan & billing** in the app shows your current plan and lets you switch between Basic and
Pro. Both changes and cancellations go through Shopify's own billing — the app never charges
you directly, and you can always double-check or cancel from **Settings → Billing** in your
Shopify admin.
