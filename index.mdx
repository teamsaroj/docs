---
title: "Porttera Bundles"
description: "Build-your-own bundles that price correctly, on any theme, with no code."
---

Porttera Bundles lets shoppers pick their own mix of products and pay one bundle price for
the set. It installs as a theme app extension, so there's no Liquid to touch and no theme
compatibility guesswork — it runs on any Online Store 2.0 theme.

<Note>
  Each item in a bundle is added to the cart as its real product variant. A Shopify Function
  discounts the group down to the bundle price behind the scenes, so inventory, fulfillment,
  and order reporting all see the real products — never a single bundled SKU.
</Note>
