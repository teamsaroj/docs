---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Create and publish a bundle in minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Add Bundles from the Shopify App Store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a bundle">
    In the app admin, pick the products that make up the bundle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the price">
    Choose a **fixed price** or a **percentage off** the combined total.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the block">
    In the theme editor, add the **Bundle** block to the product (or a dedicated bundle page).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Save and publish. Shoppers can add the whole set in one click.
  </Step>
</Steps>
