---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Install Wholesale and show partner pricing in minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Add Wholesale from the Shopify App Store. It installs as a theme app extension —
    nothing is pasted into your theme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag your partners">
    In **Customers**, add the `partner` tag to each wholesale customer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the price block">
    In the theme editor, open a **product** template and add the **Wholesale price** block
    where your price shows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a floor (optional)">
    To allow negotiation, set a global **price floor** so the assistant never offers below it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Save and publish. Tagged partners now see wholesale pricing everywhere.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <img src="/images/wholesale-setup.svg" alt="Add the Wholesale price block" />

<Check>
  Log in as a tagged partner and open a product — the wholesale price should show in place
  of the regular price, and carry through to checkout.
</Check>
