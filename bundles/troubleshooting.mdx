---
title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Real causes for the problems that actually come up, not generic advice."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The builder doesn't show on the page" icon="eye-slash">
    In the theme editor, the block only renders once at least one handle in **Bundle handles**
    matches a bundle you've actually saved. Check for:

    - A typo in the handle, or a handle copied from the wrong bundle.
    - A bundle that hasn't been saved yet — it doesn't exist until you click **Save** in the
      editor, and only then does it get a handle.
    - Trailing spaces or an extra blank line in **Bundle handles** — each line is trimmed, but
      an entirely empty line is just skipped, not an error.

    While you're editing the theme, an empty state placeholder shows in the block instead of
    nothing — that placeholder only appears in the theme editor's preview, not on the live
    page, so don't worry that it "still shows" in a private window.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The bundle pricing discount isn't active" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The app's home page shows a checklist, and one line is whether the automatic **Bundle
    pricing** discount is active. It's created automatically the first time the app loads —
    if it says not active, reload the app admin after a minute or two. If it's still not active
    after that, [get in touch](https://porttera.com/pages/contact) — it usually means the
    pricing Function hasn't finished deploying on our end, not something wrong on your store.
    Until it's active, bundle items are added to the cart at full price with no discount.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bundles aren't loading in the theme editor" icon="database">
    This usually means the metaobject definitions the app relies on got out of sync — it can
    happen after a long gap between an old install and a reinstall. Go to the app's
    **Settings** page and click **Re-create definitions**. It's safe to run any time; it only
    creates what's missing and leaves existing bundles alone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Adding a bundle to the cart fails with an error" icon="cart-shopping">
    The whole bundle is added in one request, so if any single chosen variant is out of stock
    or otherwise unavailable, Shopify rejects the entire request and nothing gets added — the
    customer sees an error message rather than a partial cart. Check stock on the specific
    variant the customer picked, not just the product overall.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The discount isn't applying, or the price looks wrong" icon="dollar-sign">
    A few things to check, in order:

    1. In **Discounts** in your Shopify admin, confirm the automatic **Bundle pricing**
       discount exists and its **Product discount (required)** checkbox is still on — turning
       it off stops the discount from running.
    2. Confirm the bundle's own price or tiers are set the way you expect, in the app admin.
    3. If a customer manually removes one item from an otherwise-complete bundle in their cart,
       the group is no longer intact, so the discount stops applying to what's left — this is
       expected, not a bug, since the remaining items are no longer "the bundle" as configured.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Subscribe & Save doesn't show on the storefront" icon="rotate">
    Subscribe & Save needs a Shopify subscriptions scope that's gated behind app review. If
    your app installation hasn't been approved for it yet, the bundle still saves normally —
    it just saves without a working subscribe option, silently, rather than failing the save.
    This resolves itself once the subscriptions scope is approved; no action needed on your
    end besides waiting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Still stuck? [Contact us](https://porttera.com/pages/contact) with the bundle's handle and
  your store URL — that's almost always enough to spot the problem right away.
</Note>
