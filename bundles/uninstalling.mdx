---
title: "Uninstalling"
description: "How to remove Porttera Bundles cleanly, and exactly what happens to your data."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Remove the theme block first">
    In **Online Store → Themes → Customize**, delete the **Bundle builder** block from any page
    or template it's on, and save. Do this before uninstalling — once the app is uninstalled,
    the pricing discount stops running and the block has nothing valid to show.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Uninstall the app">
    From your Shopify admin, go to **Settings → Apps and sales channels**, find Porttera
    Bundles, and uninstall it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets removed

- Your billing subscription cancels automatically — Shopify handles this as part of
  uninstalling, and it stops the charge on your next billing cycle.
- The app's OAuth session for your store is deleted right away.
- The small record the app keeps about your shop (plan, onboarding status) is deleted, at the
  latest, within about 48 hours, when Shopify sends the app its mandatory `shop/redact`
  cleanup webhook.

## What stays on your store

Bundle configuration — the bundles, categories, and items you set up — is stored as
**metaobjects on your own Shopify store**, not in the app's database. Uninstalling the app
doesn't touch them, so they're still there afterward. They're inert without the app (nothing
reads or writes them, and the pricing discount is gone), so they won't affect your store, but
if you want them gone too you'll need to delete them yourself from
**Settings → Custom data → Bundle** / **Bundle item** / **Bundle category** in your Shopify
admin.

Your products, variants, and orders are yours regardless — the app never owned or altered
that data, it only referenced it.

## Reinstalling later

If you reinstall, the app re-creates its metaobject definitions automatically and any bundles
you left in place should show up again in the **Bundles** list, handles intact. You'll need to
re-add the theme block and re-choose a plan, since both of those reset on uninstall.
