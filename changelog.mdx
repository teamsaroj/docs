<Update label="v1.0.0" description="2026-06-15 — Launch">
  The first public release.

  - Theme app extension "Bundle builder" — a theme-independent build-your-own-bundle block for
    any Online Store 2.0 theme
  - Fixed-price and tiered volume-discount bundles, with categories, pick counts, and
    free-gift categories
  - Always-included items and multi-bundle tabs in a single block
  - Subscribe & Save (15% off, recurring every 30 days)
  - A Shopify Function that prices each bundle at checkout, not just on the page, while
    keeping every item its own cart line
  - Bundle manager, drop-off funnel, and sales analytics in the app admin
  - Shopify App Pricing with Basic and Pro plans
</Update>
