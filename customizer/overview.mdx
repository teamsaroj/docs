Product Customizer lets shoppers design a product, preview it live on the real product image,
and save their work to reorder later. Uploaded artwork lands in your Shopify Files and is
attached to the order for fulfilment.

## Who it's for

Stores selling **personalized or made-to-order products** — apparel, signage, gifts — that
need shoppers to customize and you to receive clean, print-ready files.
