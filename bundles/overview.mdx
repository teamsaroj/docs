Porttera Bundles adds a "build your own bundle" section to your store. Shoppers pick items —
sometimes from a few categories, sometimes just whatever they want — and check out at one
bundle price instead of paying full price item by item.

## The two pricing modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fixed price" icon="tag">
    You set one price for the whole bundle (say, "any 3 items for $60"). Shoppers fill the
    slots you've defined and pay that price no matter which items they choose.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tiered / volume" icon="chart-line">
    No fixed slots — shoppers add as many items as they want, and the discount percentage
    goes up at the item counts you set (e.g. 3 items = 10% off, 5 = 15% off, 10 = 25% off).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

A discount only ever lowers the price. If a shopper's picks already cost less than the bundle
price on a fixed bundle, no discount applies — the app won't charge more than the items are
already worth.

## Why it works on any theme, with no theme code

The builder is a **Shopify theme app extension** — a block you add through the theme editor,
not a snippet pasted into `theme.liquid`. That's what makes it theme-independent: switch
themes, and you just re-add the block on the new one. Nothing breaks when a theme updates,
and nothing is left in your theme files if you remove it.

## Why the pricing is safe

A lot of bundle apps create one combined "bundle SKU," which is where inventory tracking,
reporting, and returns usually get messy. Porttera Bundles doesn't do that. Each item a
shopper picks is added to the cart as **its own real variant**, tagged with a few hidden
properties that identify which bundle it belongs to. A Shopify Function reads those
properties at checkout, confirms the full set is there, and discounts the group down to the
bundle price — while every item stays its own line in the cart, the order, and your reports.

See [How it works](/bundles/how-it-works) for the exact mechanics.
