---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Common questions about Porttera Bundles."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does it slow my store down?" icon="gauge-high">
    It shouldn't. The builder is a theme app extension with a deferred script, and nothing is
    injected into your theme's layout outside the block itself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does it inject code into my theme?" icon="code">
    No. It's a block you add in the theme editor — there's no pasted snippet in
    `theme.liquid`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I uninstall?" icon="trash">
    Your billing cancels, and the app's own records (your session, your plan) are deleted.
    Bundle configuration is stored as metaobjects on your own store, so it stays behind unless
    you remove it yourself. Full details in [Uninstalling](/bundles/uninstalling).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which themes are supported?" icon="palette">
    Any **Online Store 2.0** theme. Switching themes just means re-adding the block on the new
    one — your bundles themselves aren't tied to a theme.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a bundle discount combine with other discounts?" icon="tags">
    Yes, by default. The automatic bundle discount is set to combine with order, product, and
    shipping discounts, so a bundle can still pick up a storewide sale or a shipping promo on
    top of its own price.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is billing handled?" icon="credit-card">
    Through Shopify's own billing, under whichever plan you choose after installing. Change or
    cancel any time from Shopify's billing settings, or by uninstalling.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data safe?" icon="shield-halved">
    The app doesn't collect customer personal data at all — no names, emails, or order
    contents. It only touches products, discounts, and the metaobjects it stores your bundle
    configuration in. See the full [privacy policy](https://porttera.com/pages/privacy).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get support?" icon="life-ring">
    Use the [contact form](https://porttera.com/pages/contact) — it reaches the person who
    built the app, not a ticket queue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
